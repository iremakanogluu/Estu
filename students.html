ECTS Credit System

Eskişehir Technical University uses the ECTS system. In formal education, the defined workload and related learning outcomes in an academic year correspond to 60 ECTS. Since 1 ECTS credit corresponds to 25 hours at Eskişehir Technical University, the student workload in an academic year is 1500 hours.

Registration procedures

All candidate students who are entitled to enroll in the university can register together with the required documents for final enrollment, provided that they pay the tuition fee determined by the Cabinet Decision at the beginning of each academic year.

General Admission Conditions

For Turkish students; In Turkey, central placement and special talent exams are applied in the admission of students to associate and undergraduate programs of Higher Education Institutions.

Academic Counseling

A faculty member is appointed to each of the students enrolled in Eskişehir Technical University to provide consultancy in every subject within the framework of the "Eskişehir Technical University Academic Counseling Directive".

International Programs

At Eskişehir Technical University, student and staff mobility is carried out to European countries and many countries in different geographies of the world with Erasmus and Erasmus + exchange programs.
